Who We Are

AQUAFLOW is a visionary social enterprise dedicated to transforming water accessibility through innovation, sustainability, and community empowerment. We combine cutting-edge purification technology with eco-conscious business models to provide clean, safe, and affordable water across Africa and beyond.

We’re not just a water company. We’re a movement redefining how communities interact with this essential resource.

Our Mission & Vision

  • Mission: To democratize access to clean water through sustainable innovation and eliminate water scarcity.
  • Vision: To become Africa's most impactful water infrastructure platform by 2030, ensuring no child walks more than 500m for clean water.

Core Values

We are guided by five pillars:
Quality: Rigorous testing for purity.
Sustainability: Minimizing environmental footprint.
Innovation: Pioneering new ways to deliver water.
Integrity: Transparent and ethical in all we do.
Community: Empowering those we serve.
